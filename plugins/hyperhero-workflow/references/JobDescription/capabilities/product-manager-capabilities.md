# Product Manager Technical Capabilities Reference

> Sources:
> - `sample/startup-business-analyst/agents/startup-analyst.md`
> - `sample/business-analytics/agents/business-analyst.md`

## Market Sizing & Opportunity Analysis

- **TAM/SAM/SOM calculations**: Bottom-up and top-down methodologies
- **Market research**: Credible data sources, industry reports, public companies
- **Value theory approaches**: New market categories, market evolution
- **Industry-specific templates**: SaaS, marketplace, consumer, B2B, fintech
- **Growth projections**: Market evolution analysis, triangulation validation

## Financial Modeling & Unit Economics

- **Revenue modeling**: Cohort-based projections, forecasting techniques
- **Unit economics**: CAC, LTV, payback period, retention modeling
- **Financial models**: 3-5 year projections with scenarios
- **Cash flow**: Burn rate, runway analysis, efficiency metrics
- **Business model optimization**: Pricing, packaging, monetization strategies

## Competitive Analysis & Strategy

- **Porter's Five Forces**: Application and analysis
- **Blue Ocean Strategy**: Framework application
- **Competitive positioning**: Differentiation, market landscape mapping
- **Competitive intelligence**: Gathering, analysis, sustainable advantage
- **Go-to-market strategy**: Market entry approaches, partnership strategy

## KPI Framework & Metrics

- **North Star metrics**: Identification and tracking
- **OKR framework**: Objectives and Key Results development
- **Balanced scorecard**: Implementation and management
- **Performance measurement**: System design, metric hierarchy
- **Industry benchmarks**: KPI benchmarking against standards

## Customer & Market Analytics

- **Customer segmentation**: Persona development, profiling
- **Churn prediction**: Prevention strategies, retention modeling
- **Market sizing**: TAM analysis and validation
- **Product-market fit**: Analysis and validation methodologies
- **Customer journey mapping**: Funnel optimization, VoC analysis

## Data Visualization & Storytelling

- **Dashboard design**: Tableau, Power BI, Looker, executive dashboards
- **Data storytelling**: Frameworks, narrative development
- **Visual analytics**: Pattern recognition, insight discovery
- **Executive presentations**: Design, stakeholder communication
- **Accessibility**: Inclusive data visualization standards

## Startup Metrics (by Business Model)

### SaaS Metrics
- MRR/ARR, NDR (Net Dollar Retention), CAC payback
- Logo churn vs revenue churn
- Magic number, Rule of 40

### Marketplace Metrics
- GMV, take rate, liquidity
- Supply/demand balance
- Cross-network effects

### Consumer Metrics
- DAU/MAU, retention cohorts
- Virality (K-factor), engagement
- ARPU, conversion rates

### B2B Metrics
- ACV, sales efficiency, win rate
- Deal cycle length, pipeline velocity
- Expansion revenue

## Requirements Gathering & Analysis

- **User research**: Interview techniques, survey design
- **Requirements documentation**: Functional/non-functional requirements
- **Prioritization frameworks**: MoSCoW, RICE, Impact-Effort matrix
- **User story writing**: Acceptance criteria, definition of done
- **Stakeholder management**: Communication, expectation alignment

## Strategic Planning

- **Roadmap development**: Quarterly/annual planning
- **Resource allocation**: Team planning, budget optimization
- **Risk assessment**: Identification, mitigation strategies
- **Decision frameworks**: Data-driven decision making
- **Scenario planning**: Conservative, base, optimistic modeling
